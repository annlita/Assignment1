About Me!

Hello there! I'm Annlita Joshy Aykara, a second year international student at Ontario Tech University. Currently, I'm doing my Bachelors in Networking and IT Security. I'm from India, but I was born and raised in Oman. Since my childhood, I have always had an ardent love for the subject of computers and therefore I aspire to establish my career in the field of Computer/IT Security. In this era, we are more dependent on technology than ever before through online banking, E-commerce, hybrid teaching, etc, especially during these COVID times, technology proved to be a boon for us making it indispensable to us. With the advancement of technology in every walk of life; the privacy and security of our personal data and cyberspace is essential. Growing up, I have noticed online adversaries evolve in terms of severity and attack mechanisms. Pursuing a career in this subject I strive to build a safe and secure internet environment for everyone.
